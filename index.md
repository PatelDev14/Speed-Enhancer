# Privacy Policy - YouTube Speed Enhancer

**Last Updated:** July 2026

## Overview
YouTube Speed Enhancer is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

## Information We Collect
YouTube Speed Enhancer does **NOT** collect any personal information. Specifically:
- We do NOT collect browsing history
- We do NOT collect video data
- We do NOT collect user identification information
- We do NOT use analytics or tracking services
- We do NOT send data to any external servers

## How We Use Information
All data is stored locally on your device using browser storage. This includes:
- Your preferred playback speed setting
- This data never leaves your device

## Data Storage
- All settings are stored in your browser's local storage
- This data is not synchronized across devices
- This data is not uploaded anywhere
- Clearing your browser data will clear extension settings

## Third-Party Services
YouTube Speed Enhancer does NOT use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools.

## Your Rights
- You have full control over your data
- You can reset settings anytime by uninstalling and reinstalling the extension
- You can contact us with privacy concerns
